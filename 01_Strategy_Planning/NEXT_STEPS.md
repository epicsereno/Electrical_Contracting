# 🗺️ Next Steps: VoltMaster Electrical Contracting

This document outlines the strategic initiatives to modernize and expand VoltMaster's electrical contracting operations.

## 🚀 Phase 1: Operational Modernization
- [ ] **Field Reporting App:** Implement a mobile platform for real-time field reporting, RFI submissions, and job-site photo documentation. 📱
- [ ] **Fleet Telematics:** Upgrade all service vehicles with GPS tracking and telematics for optimized dispatch and maintenance. 🚚
- [ ] **BIM Integration:** Invest in and train the estimating team on Building Information Modeling (BIM) software for 3D design-build projects. 🏗️

## 🔋 Phase 2: Service Expansion
- [ ] **VoltMaster EV:** Launch a specialized division dedicated to residential and commercial Electric Vehicle (EV) charger installations. 🔋
- [ ] **Solar Integration:** Develop a comprehensive service offering for commercial solar-integrated electrical systems. ☀️
- [ ] **Industrial Audits:** Launch an "Energy Efficiency Audit" service to help industrial clients optimize power consumption and power factor. 🔌

## 📈 Phase 3: Safety & Human Capital
- [ ] **Arc Flash Certification:** Ensure 100% of journeyman electricians complete advanced NFPA 70E Arc Flash Safety certification. 🛡️
- [ ] **Apprenticeship Program:** Formalize an internal 4-year apprenticeship program to build a sustainable talent pipeline. 🎓
- [ ] **Inventory Control:** Implement a barcode-based asset tracking system in the warehouse to reduce tool and material shrinkage. 📉
- [ ] **Strategic Bidding:** Target and secure "Tier 1 Subcontractor" status with three major regional commercial developers. 🤝
