## Status Definitions:
*   **Draft:** Estimate in progress.
*   **Submitted:** Proposal sent, waiting for response.
*   **Pending:** Active negotiation.
*   **Awarded:** Move to `03_Operations_Projects/01_Active_Jobs`.
*   **Lost:** Move to `02_Estimating_Bidding/03_Lost_Bids`.
*   **On Hold:** Postponed by client.
