# Company Electrical Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

**Purpose:** To maintain the high standards of quality and safety established over 30 years of experience. These standards are mandatory for all 10 employees on every jobsite.

---

## 1. Rough-In Standards
*   **Box Heights (Unless spec'd otherwise):**
    *   Switches: 48" to center.
    *   Receptacles: 18" to center.
    *   Countertop Outlets: 42" to center.
*   **Box Mounting:** Every box must be level and secure. Use a bracket or support if mounting to metal studs—no "floated" boxes.
*   **Conduit Supports:** One-hole straps or click-its must be within 12" of every box and every 10' thereafter (exceeding code where necessary for a professional look).

## 2. Wiring & Labeling
*   **Circuit ID:** Every home-run must be labeled with the Panel and Circuit Number (e.g., PNL-A #12) at both ends.
*   **Color Coding:**
    *   120/240V: Black, Red, Blue, White (Neutral), Green (Ground).
    *   277/480V: Brown, Orange, Yellow, Gray (Neutral), Green (Ground).
*   **Wire Management:** All wires in junction boxes must be neatly folded (pigtails) with at least 6" of free conductor. No "birds' nests."

## 3. Panel & Trim Work
*   **Panel Neatness:** Conductors must be routed along the edges of the tub at 90-degree angles. Use zip-ties sparingly but effectively.
*   **Labeling:** Typed or neatly printed panel schedules are required before final inspection. No handwritten scribbles on the door.
*   **Device Alignment:** Receptacles and switches must be perfectly vertical. Use a level; don't eye-ball it.

## 4. Jobsite Professionalism
*   **The "Daily Clean":** No jobsite is left with wire scraps, knockouts, or cardboard at the end of the shift. Use the shop vac every day.
*   **Client Interaction:** Refer all change order requests or technical questions from the client/GC to the Foreman.
*   **Safety PPE:** Hard hats and safety glasses are non-negotiable from the moment you exit the truck.

## 5. Tool & Material Management
*   **Truck Stock:** Every Friday, trucks are to be inventoried and restocked for Monday morning.
*   **Tool Care:** Any damaged power tools or ladders must be tagged "RED - DO NOT USE" and reported to the office immediately.
