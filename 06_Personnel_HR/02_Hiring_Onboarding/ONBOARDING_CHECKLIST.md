## 1. Documentation & Legal (Office)
- [ ] Employment Application completed.
- [ ] W-4 Federal Tax Form signed.
- [ ] I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification (with copies of ID).
- [ ] Direct Deposit Authorization Form.
- [ ] Emergency Contact Information.
- [ ] Signed Employee Handbook / Company Policies.

## 2. Safety & Training
- [ ] Review of Company Safety Manual.
- [ ] PPE Issued: Hard Hat, Safety Glasses, High-Vis Vest, Gloves.
- [ ] Review of Arc Flash / Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) procedures.
- [ ] Orientation on Tailgate Safety Meetings.
- [ ] Copies of existing certifications (OSHA 10/30, Lift Cert, etc.).

## 3. Tools & Equipment
- [ ] Review of Required Tool List (Employee-provided).
- [ ] Company-issued tools (if any): ____________________
- [ ] Truck / Vehicle assignment (if applicable).
- [ ] Key / Gate Code access.

## 4. Field Orientation
- [ ] Introduction to Team / Foreman.
- [ ] Review of "The Company Way" (Standard Specs).
- [ ] Training on Daily Field Report entry.
- [ ] Timecard submission procedure.
