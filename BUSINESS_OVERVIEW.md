# Electrical Contracting Business Overview

## Business Model: C10 Electrical Contractor
**Scale:** Small Business (10 Employees)
**Experience:** 30+ Years Industry Experience

## Directory Structure Guide

### 01_Accounting_Finance
- **Payable:** Vendor invoices and payments.
- **Receivable:** Customer billing and collections.
- **Payroll:** Weekly timecards and certified payroll (if applicable).
- **Tax_Documents:** Corporate and employment tax records.

### 02_Estimating_Bidding
- **Active_Bids:** Current proposals in progress.
- **Awarded_Projects:** Projects ready to transition to Operations.
- **Lost_Bids:** Archive of past bids for historical data.
- **Vendor_Pricing:** Current material pricing from suppliers.

### 03_Operations_Projects
- **Active_Jobs:** Project management files (Submittals, RFIs, Change Orders).
- **Completed_Jobs:** Close-out documents and warranties.
- **Scheduling:** Manpower and project timelines.
- **Safety_OSHA:** Tailgate meetings, Toolbox talks, and compliance.

### 04_Human_Resources
- **Employee_Files:** Personnel records, onboarding, and training certifications.
- **Hiring_Onboarding:** Documentation for new hires.

### 05_Fleet_Equipment
- **Vehicle_Maintenance:** Logs for service trucks.
- **Tool_Inventory:** Tracking of major equipment and specialized tools.
- **Material_Inventory:** Warehouse and truck stock management.

### 06_Legal_Licensing
- **C10_License:** State Board licensing records.
- **Insurance_Policies:** General Liability, Workers Comp, and Auto.
- **Contracts_Agreements:** Master service agreements and subcontracts.

### 07_Marketing_Sales
- **Branding_Assets:** Logos, business cards, and project photos.
- **Lead_Generation:** Customer inquiries and referrals.
